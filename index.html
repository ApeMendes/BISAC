Why BISAC?

Innovative Technology

BISAC integrates low-cost, disposable, and biodegradable sensors capable of detecting lactose concentration in liquid or solid samples in approximately one minute.

  • Zein-Coated Sensors:

    Utilizing a biodegradable protein derived from corn for eco-friendly disposal.

  • Quantitative Results:

    Unlike competitors that only say "Yes/No", BISAC tells you exactly how much lactose is present (mg/g).

  • Smart App Integration:

    Tracks daily intake and customizes alerts based on your specific sensitivity level.
